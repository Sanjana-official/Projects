Introduction

A campus is traditionally the land on which a college or university and related institutional buildings are situated. Modern campuses often include libraries, lecture halls, residence halls, and parks.

History

The concept of a campus originated in the United States, evolving from early colonial colleges into expansive educational environments.

Campus Life

Campus life includes academics, social interaction, extracurricular activities, and residential experiences.
