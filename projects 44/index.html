Chocolate Cake 🍰

Soft, rich, and delicious homemade cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Add eggs and milk
  4. Pour into baking pan
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
  6. Let it cool and serve
